About

MCAST aims to provide accessible vocational and professional education and training with an international focus, responding to individual and economic needs. Established in 2001, it is Malta's leading institution for vocational education, offering over 190 full-time and 300 part-time courses across six institutes and the Gozo Campus. The institutes focus on delivering specialized programmes while engaging with stakeholders to address local economic needs. MCAST's collaboration with industry ensures program relevance, supporting SMEs through apprenticeships and tailored training. The College aims to expand MG2i's offerings and enhance international initiatives through professional development and lifelong learning programmes.
